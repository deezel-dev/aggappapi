About

FlixAcademy is a service for Teachers, Parents and Students looking to use Movies to supplement education.

Find movies and videos using the FREE FlixAcademy.com search engine. Access FlixREMIX Interactive Lesson Plans that display and optionally speak expert commentary and quiz questions during Teachable Moments in the movie.

Get paid for creating your own FlixREMIX Interactive Lesson Plans, just by filling out a simple spreadsheet!
