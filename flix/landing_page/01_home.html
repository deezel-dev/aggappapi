FlixAcademy
Don't just watch a movie... Watch. And Learn!

FlixAcademy helps Teachers, Parents and Students use movies for educational purposes. Our FlixREMIX app adds Interactive Lesson Plans that play on a Second Screen mobile device while the movie is playing on the Big Screen! See and hear expert commentary during Teachable Moments and answer quiz questions based on the current scene in the movie!
